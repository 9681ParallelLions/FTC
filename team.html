About Our Team

Although our number may be lower than most, team 7890 is completely new. All of the previous members have moved up to FRC and this year, we had the chance to build our team from the ground up. Our team is composed of freshmen and sophomores. We meet on Fridays in the lab at our school, Horace Mann.This year 7890 hopes to perform to the best of our abilities in all competitions and to put maximum effort into all of our ventures as a team.
