Our Outreach!

For our outreach, we focused a lot on helping the community within reach of us. Since many of our members are First Lego League veterans, we decided that mentoring FLL would be the best intersection of building community and promoting STEM and robotics. We are a big help to Horace Mann's FLL Team, the Basement Cubs. We help introduce and guide them through not only the basics of engineering, but also the first steps in researching, how to reach out to the general public, and the FIRST core values. We also helped George Jackson Academy, an elementary/middle school that has been trying to set up a robotics team, but lacks the resources. The GJA FLL Team comes to Horace Mann every so often to use our resources and ask for our feedback and suggestions on anything from chassis to programming to team organization
